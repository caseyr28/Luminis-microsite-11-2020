Our Approach

Health care that's just easier

Great care is more than great medicine. It's about understanding you and what matters most to you. At Luminis Health, every day is about making people feel their best. We're by your side, making sure you have what you need to get involved and take control of your health.

Find out

How do we do this?

We take down barriers to health, make care easier, and make sure everyone has access to the tools they need to live a healthy life. You are our priority and we make sure you feel it.

And high-quality care is right here

Here are few ways we're making care better and more accessible in our community:

Applying for a Certificate of Need to add labor and delivery services at Doctors Community Medical Center

Opening the J. Kent McNew Family Medical Center to offer inpatient mental health services in our region

Offering cardiac surgery to keep patients closer to home

Expanding research, clinical trials and Graduate Medical Education programs to offer the latest in advanced medicine

Offering expanded access to telehealth for convenient expert care

We know you might have a few questions

Here are a few answers to shed light on what this means for you.

How come Doctors Community Medical Center and Anne Arundel Medical Center came together?

Well, we have a strong and shared dedication to the communities and people we serve. And by creating a broader health system, we knew we could do more for our patients and communities, like giving them what they need and deserve when it comes to their health.

Can I keep my doctor?

Yes — you can keep seeing your favorite doctors, providers and care teams, just like normal. And now that they're part of a larger system, that means you get care for more specialties and conditions. Also, your insurance coverage won't change because of our new system.

So, what will change?

You'll see our new look and our new logo. But you'll also hear more about how we're getting to know our community and your needs a bit better, and how we're providing the care you deserve.

Over the next two years, we're taking the best of both health systems and upgrading our programs and services. We're adding more coordinated care and improving access, making sure you can get what you need closer to home.

We're bringing everything together by honoring our histories while also being aspirational about what we create for the future.

Why did you change your name to Luminis Health?

We needed a new name that shares our optimism and promise to care for our communities. Our employees, providers, volunteers and community members all helped us choose Luminis Health, by sharing their thoughts in surveys, research and interviews. Go to About Us to learn more about the inspiration behind the name.
